I am an Assistant Professor in the Departments of Knowledge Intergration and Sociology & Legal Studies at University of Waterloo. I run a lab that does social networks research and computational social science research on a range of issues related to science and technology policy. You can read a bit more about my work here

This site contains information about my research, teaching, and supervision at the University of Waterloo. If you want to learn more about the computational social science and social networks research my lab is doing, please visit the NetLab website.

Download my CV.

In addition to my normal research and teaching life, I have the privilege of working with Eric Kennedy on Science Outside the Lab North (SOtL North). SOtL North is a week long intensive course in science policy held annually in Ottawa and Montréal. Each course cohort is ~ 14 graduate students from science and engineering disciplines across Canada.
