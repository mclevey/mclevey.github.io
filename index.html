I am an Assistant Professor in the Departments of Knowledge Intergration and Sociology & Legal Studies at University of Waterloo. I run a lab that does social networks research and computational social science research on a range of issues related to science and technology policy.

This site contains information about my research, teaching, and supervision at the University of Waterloo. If you want to learn more about the computational social science and social networks research my lab is doing, please visit the NetLab website.
