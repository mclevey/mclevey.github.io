Industrial Development and Eco-Tourisms

Mark Stoddart, Alice Mattoni, and John McLevey
Palgrave MacMillan, 2020

Is co-existence possible between oil exploration and nature conservation? This book examines the tensions and potential compatibilities between industrial development and eco-tourism in northern coastal societies, with comparative case studies from Norway, Scotland, and Newfoundland and Labrador.
